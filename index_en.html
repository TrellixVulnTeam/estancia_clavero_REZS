About us

In the Valley of Traslasierra, among the Great Sierras de Córdoba, lies the Estancia Clavero, in territoroes belonging to the Pedanía Nono. With a size of 200 hectares, the terrain was converted into a mountain hotel, with restaurant and a tea room. It is located in the Camino de las Altas Cumbres, ten kilometers before the intersection that divides Mina Clavero and Nono. The inn has a rich history, which is characterized by the cordiality bringed by it's host, Juan Pérez. It's 138km away from Córdoba Capital, 12 from Mina Clavero's center, and around 700 meters from the Camino de las Altas Cumbres, placed in a valley at the start of the Cerro "La Esperanza", which grants absolute and exclusive peace and tranquility. In other words, a great alternative to rest and relax.
